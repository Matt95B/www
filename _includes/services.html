Professional Services - The D6 Framework

Define

Align on clear business outcomes, scope, and measurable success criteria before delivery begins.

Discover

Assess the current state across people, process, and technology to validate requirements and identify gaps.

Design

Architect a secure, scalable future-state solution aligned to business and operational needs.

Develop

Build and configure the solution with best-practice standards, automation, and integration.

Deploy

Validate, pilot, and transition the solution into production with controlled rollout and governance.

Drive Value

Enable adoption, optimise operations, and ensure measurable business value is realised post-deployment.
