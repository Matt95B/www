# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: {{ site.time | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}_

BeaugTech ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information.

---

## 1. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including:

- Name
- Email address
- Company name
- Job title
- Phone number
- Information submitted via contact forms
- Business information provided during consulting engagements

We may also collect limited technical data such as:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Website usage analytics data

---

## 2. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information when you:

- Submit a contact form
- Email us directly
- Engage us for consulting services
- Subscribe to updates (if applicable)
- Interact with our website

We may also collect information through cookies or analytics tools.

---

## 3. Purpose of Collection

We collect and use personal information to:

- Respond to enquiries
- Provide consulting services
- Manage client relationships
- Issue invoices and administer contracts
- Improve website performance and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

---

## 4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose personal information to:

- Professional advisers (legal, accounting)
- Cloud service providers used to operate our business
- Technology vendors where required for service delivery
- Government authorities where required by law

Where third parties are engaged, reasonable steps are taken to ensure they handle personal information securely.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

This may include:

- Secure cloud platforms
- Access controls
- Encrypted communications where applicable

However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for:

- Providing services
- Meeting contractual obligations
- Legal and accounting requirements

When no longer required, information is securely deleted or de-identified.

---

## 7. Access and Correction

You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable law.

---

## 8. Overseas Disclosure

Some service providers may store data outside Australia.

Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients comply with privacy obligations consistent with Australian law.

---

## 9. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to understand usage patterns and improve user experience.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

---

## 10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

---

## 11. Complaints

If you believe your privacy has been breached, please contact us in writing.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

---

## 12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
